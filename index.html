Age: 18

Reference

Solfege Map

D: Do (0), d: da (1), R: Re (2), r: ri (3), M: Mi (4), F: Fa (5), f: fi (6), S: So (7), s: su (8), L: La (9), l: le (10), T: Ti (11)

Timing Modifiers

  • :D → Half-beat
  • ::D → Quarter-beat
  • _D → Double beat
  • __D → Quad beat

Octave Markers

  • / or !: High octave (+12)
  • \\ or ¡¡: Extra low (-24)
  • // or !!: Extra high (+24)
  • ~: Reset to natural

Voice Markers

  • `: Bell voice
  • *: Whistle voice

Nink Number Literals

Trigger: *:D`:T — this signals that a number follows.

Digit Mapping (whistle notes only)

  • *d → 0
  • *R → 1
  • *r → 2
  • *M → 3
  • *F → 4
  • *f → 5
  • *S → 6
  • *s → 7
  • *L → 8
  • *l → 9

Examples

  • *:D`:T *R → 1
  • *:D`:T *Rd → 10
  • *:D`:T *lMF → 934

Only whistle notes are valid. If bell notes are used after the number marker, it’s considered invalid.
